Common questions

you asked, I ANSWERED

Everything you need to know before we work together. Still have something on your mind?

Just reach out →
  • How does the process work?
    +
    We start with a discovery call to understand your workflow, pain points, and goals. From there I scope the project, send a proposal, and once we're aligned — I get building. You'll get regular check-ins and a full walkthrough before delivery.
  • How long does a project take?
    +
    It depends on scope — a simple portal can be done in a few days, while a full company backend might take 2–3 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline in the proposal so there are no surprises.
  • Do you offer revisions?
    +
    Yes — every project includes revision rounds. I want the system to actually work for you, so we don't close out until you're happy with it.
  • What do you need from me to get started?
    +
    Just your time for a discovery call and access to your current Notion workspace if you have one. The more context you give me, the better I can build for you.
  • Can you work with my existing Notion setup?
    +
    Absolutely. I can audit, restructure, and build on top of what you already have — or start fresh if that makes more sense. We'll decide together during discovery.
